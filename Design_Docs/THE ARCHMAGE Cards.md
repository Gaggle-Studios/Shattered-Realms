## INSIGHT GENERATION SOURCES

Understanding how the Archmage generates Insight is critical for playing and balancing this hero. Here is every source of Insight in the deck:

**Passive (Mana Conservation):** Unspent mana at end of opponent's turn converts to Insight.

**Minion Inspire Effects:** Mana Sprite (1/turn), Rune Scholar (1/turn), Ethereal Scholar (2/turn), Leyline Guardian (3/turn), Arcane Sovereign (2/turn, on Surge), Infinite Wyrm (3/turn), Dreamweaver Sphinx (2/turn), Nexus of All Spells (4/turn), Omniscience Avatar (5/turn).

**Spell-Generated Insight:** Prestidigitation (1), Arcane Recovery (1), Deep Research (2), Thought Erasure (2), Arcane Denial (3), Absorb (3), Mindshatter (variable), Stolen Knowledge (2), Leyline Tap (5), Knowledge Overload (5), Overwhelming Intellect (variable), Arcane Supremacy (10), Omniscience (15).

**Enchantment-Generated Insight:** Insight Engine (1 per instant), Counterspell Nexus (2 per counter), Arcane Singularity (2 per spell), Leyline Network (2/turn), Arcane Omnipotence (2 per spell).

**Upgrade-Generated Insight:** Insight Siphon (combat damage), Chrono Core (2/turn), Mind Lattice (3/turn), Infinity Matrix (5/turn).

**Minion Triggered Insight:** Thought Siphon (3 per counter), Arcane Archivist (1 on play), Temporal Adept (variable on play), Mindbreaker (variable on play).
